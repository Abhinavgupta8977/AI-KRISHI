Indian Government Policies for Farmers

Policy 1: Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN):

This is a direct income support scheme that provides financial assistance to small and marginal farmers.

Policy 2: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY):

Crop insurance scheme to protect farmers against crop loss due to natural disasters.

Policy 3: Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme:

Provides farmers with credit to meet their agricultural and other financial needs.

Policy 4: Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY):

A scheme to promote agricultural development by providing funds to states for various agricultural projects.

Policy 5: National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA):

Aims to promote sustainable agriculture through various initiatives like soil health card scheme and organic farming.

Policy 6: Soil Health Card Scheme:

Provides farmers with information about the nutrient status of their soil to improve crop productivity.

Policy 7: Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY):

Promotes organic farming and certification among farmers.

Policy 8: National Horticulture Mission (NHM)

Supports the horticulture sector through various programs aimed at increasing production and income of farmers.
